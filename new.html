This is the newest addition to my ticket collection (thank God they sent a physical one). If I had to list the best gigs I ever went to, and which had a big influence on me, Underworld would definitely compete for the top positions. I had known the band for years (thanks to Trainspotting), and I always loved them. They were my gateway to dance music. But at this gig, I discovered them from a different perspective. I was feeling a bit down before it, and afterwards, I left with my inner battery overflowing and feeling so inspired to do things. It brought back my joy for creativity, and I lost myself again in artsy experiments. I could relate a lot to the band's creative process too, even though they do music and I do visual arts. So, for me, this ticket represents a new chapter in my creative endeavours, a refreshed start. A new-ish me. — Dominyka
